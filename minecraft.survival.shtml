Minecraft in Survival mode Tutorial

Minecraft isn't a game of just skill, luck also plays a part. Here I will tell you how to actually play the game

The first thing you need to do when playing in survival mode is get some wood. You get it by holding it down. Once it is removed from the screen, look for it in your inventory. If it is not there, get closer to the wood and hold it down. From your inventory, put the wood in your craft:

---picture of wood in inventory and craft table ---

The wood from the inventory becomes planks. Once you have 4 planks of wood, put them in each corner of your craft -- and you will make a crafting table:

--picture of craft and a crafting table--

Making a crafting table is very important. You craft only has 4 spaces, but the crafting table has 9 spaces to combine ingredients. With more spaces to combine more ingredients, you will be able to make more complex items that will help you later on.

To use the crafting table you need to put it down. But you will need to build a shelter first before you put it down. Look for three sheep and two cows -- kill them all! Now put your crafting table down and build a shelter with your remaining planks of wood. You need a shelter to protect yourself at night from monsters. If you run out of planks, get some more wood and make more planks. Once inside your shelter, you can finally put down your crafting table.

At this point, you might have noticed that you have been taking damage. When this happens, quickly eat the food you got by killing the sheeps and cows. The damage you got is because you are hungry.
