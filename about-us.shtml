About UNIX me

I use SOLARIS UNIX at work, which is now distributed by ORACLE. It's a great, stable Enterprise version, but is missing some of the neat commands of LINUX/Debian/UBUNTU.

I am a pure UBUNTU person at home. This is where I run my webserver and my other projects.

I really like how easy it is to get, install and update on UBUNTU. It helps that I have root access on UBUNTU and understandable why I would not have root access at work in a production environment -- you can't have a production environment opened up for any type of executable content.

I will be adding content in the weeks to come. I will attempt to build an ad-free UNIX and webserver guide.
