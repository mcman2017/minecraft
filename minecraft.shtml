Minecraft Tips

  1. When you first spawn, get some wood and make a crafting table. Then, make an axe and get more wood.

  2. If you find any sheep, kill them because if you kill three sheep you can make a bed and sleep thru the night when the monsters spawn.

  3. If you can't find any sheep, quickly make a house so that the monsters can't get you.

  4. The first thing you should mine is coal so you can make torches.

  5. If you see red stone, be careful because that means hot lava is near by.

  6. Make a bucket and put water in it. Pour this over the lava and you will make obsidian. Only a diamond pick axe can break the obsidian.
